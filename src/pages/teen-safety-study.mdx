## Research Study Title

Understanding the online security and privacy experiences of teens

## Purpose of the research study

We want to interview teens about their security and privacy experiences online.
Our goal is to work with teens to help them be safer online.

## Study Investigators

- Daniel Zappala, Brigham Young University, zappala@cs.byu.edu
- Saba Iqbal, Brigham Young University

## Eligibility

- Teens 14-17 years old
- Living in the U.S., speak English

## What you will do

1. Parents take a short survey and approve your participation.
1. Teens take a short survey and qualify.
1. Selected teens complete a 1 hour Zoom interview

Teens will receive $35 upon completing the interview
